Prescience Timeline

History of Our Company

January 2018 - Prescience Produce LLC begins a tiny gourmet mushroom farm serving Farmers' Markets in Western Kentucky



May 2019 - Partnership with Magney Legacy Ridge Farm yields a bumper harvest of mushrooms and specialty produce.



March 2021 - Prescience Produce LLC wins West Kentucky Innovation Challenge through Sprocket Inc. for agriculture records software idea.


May 2022 - Partnership with Codefi and the beginning of code creation for the innovative record keeping App, SeedStory.


January 2023 - SeedStory is launched as a public beta to the Android and Apple AppStores

I. What Is Prescience Produce?
1/29/2019

I'm Produce Pat.  Many people literally call me that.  
Prescience Produce LLC is my company.

I started Prescience Produce because I wanted to own a business. That's the easiest way to say it anyway.  What I really wanted was an R&D department for growing produce.  It turns out to justify have an R&D department, you have to have a company that does things that warrant researching and developing first.  That's why we're here. 

If you're wondering about the name, straightforwardly, it's a Dune reference.  Frank Herbert's Dune is my favorite series of novels.  They're great and if you haven't read them, you should.  Dune doesn't have so much to do with produce, but it does have a lot to do with ecology.  This emphasis on ecology really resonates with me and that mindset influences the things I choose to grow and how I choose to grow them.  

Dune also involves a lot of looking forward into the future, a trait the novels call "prescience."  While I won't claim to grow or even know of any "spice" that does that, I do spend a lot of time envisioning the future of food.  I am not sure the future looks as bland and sterile as its popular depiction.  Given environmental stability and time, natural systems tend towards complexity. It's not unreasonable to envision the people of the future eating fruits and vegetables we've not yet heard of. 

My goal is to seek those foods out and work on turning them into viable crops and productive ecological systems.  To accomplish this research, exploration, selective breeding will be necessary.  Prescience Produce is the vine on which the fruit of this labor will grow.
